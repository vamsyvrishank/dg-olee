1.1  Linear Algebra
     - Vectors, matrices, tensors
     - Matrix multiplication
     - Dot product and projections
     - Eigenvalues and eigenvectors
     - Matrix decomposition (LU, QR, SVD)
     - Rank, null space, column space
     Book: Mathematics for ML (Ch 2-4), ESL (Appendix)

1.2  Calculus & Optimization
     - Derivatives and partial derivatives
     - Chain rule (foundation of backprop)
     - Gradient — what it is, what direction it points
     - Hessian matrix (second derivatives)
     - Convex vs non-convex functions
     - Local vs global minima
     Book: Mathematics for ML (Ch 5-7)

1.3  Probability & Statistics
     - Random variables, distributions
     - Expectation, variance, covariance
     - Bayes theorem
     - MLE (Maximum Likelihood Estimation)
     - MAP (Maximum a Posteriori)
     - Law of large numbers, CLT
     - Hypothesis testing, p-values
     - Confidence intervals
     Book: ISLR (Ch 1), Mathematics for ML (Ch 6)

1.4  Information Theory
     - Entropy
     - KL Divergence
     - Cross-entropy (used in classification loss)
     - Mutual information
     Book: Bishop (Ch 1), ESL (Ch 9)
